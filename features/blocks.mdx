Blocks is an automation layer that sits atop Structured's data Harbor. With Blocks, you can set up automated actions that happen when certain data conditions are met, making your work faster, more efficient, and more reliable.

## Get started!

Automate confidently, knowing your workflows respect and preserve your pre-defined data model's integrity.
