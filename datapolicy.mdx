At Structured, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users' data. We understand the importance of protecting your information and have implemented comprehensive security measures and data policies to ensure your safety and peace of mind.

### Below are the key aspects of our robust security framework:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Secure Data Storage 🔐" defaultOpen>
    We store your data in highly secure data centers that adhere to the highest
    standards of physical and digital security. Our data storage facilities are
    equipped with advanced security measures, including access controls,
    surveillance systems, and intrusion detection systems, to prevent
    unauthorized access or breaches.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Access Control 🛡️" defaultOpen>
    Access to your data is strictly controlled and limited to authorized
    personnel only. We employ role-based access control mechanisms to ensure
    that users can only access the data necessary for their roles, reducing the
    risk of unauthorized access or data leaks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regular Audits and Monitoring 🕵️‍♂️" defaultOpen>
    We conduct regular security audits and monitoring to detect and prevent any
    potential security threats or breaches. Our dedicated security team
    continuously monitors our systems for any suspicious activity and takes
    proactive measures to mitigate risks and strengthen our defenses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Compliance with Data Regulations 📜" defaultOpen>
    We are committed to complying with relevant data protection regulations,
    including GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA (if applicable). Our policies and practices
    are designed to ensure that your data rights are protected and respected,
    and that we operate in accordance with the highest standards of data privacy
    and security.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transparent Privacy Policy 📃" defaultOpen>
    Our [privacy policy](https://www.structuredlabs.io/privacy-policy) outlines
    how we collect, use, and protect your data. We are transparent about our
    data practices and provide clear information about your rights and options
    regarding your personal information. We regularly update our privacy policy
    to reflect any changes in our data practices or regulatory requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="User Education and Training 🎓" defaultOpen>
    We provide ongoing education and training to our employees to ensure they
    understand and adhere to our security policies and procedures. We also offer
    resources and guidance to help users enhance their own security practices,
    such as tips for creating strong passwords and recognizing phishing
    attempts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Incident Response and Communication 🚨" defaultOpen>
    In the event of a security incident or data breach, we have established
    incident response procedures to quickly and effectively respond to the
    situation. We prioritize transparency and timely communication with affected
    users, providing updates and guidance on steps they can take to protect
    themselves.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

At Structured, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and privacy to safeguard your data. If you have any questions or concerns about our security practices, please don't hesitate to [contact us](https://www.structuredlabs.io/contact). Your trust and confidence are of utmost importance to us, and we will continue to work tirelessly to earn and maintain them.
