Structured is a managed, cloud-hosted platform for building, deploying, and sharing Python-based analytics apps. Powered by the open-source [Preswald](https://github.com/StructuredLabs/preswald) framework, you can import GitHub repositories, customize your apps with your own branding, and deploy them in seconds—all in a secure and scalable environment. Collaborate with your team, share your apps effortlessly, set up authentication, and turn raw data into actionable, visually engaging reports and dashboards.

Structured makes it easy to go from idea to production without needing to manage infrastructure.
